Towards the end of High School, I got into circuits and thats when I decided to pursue Computer Engineering in college. I enjoy sharing my projects and experiences on YouTube, where I post build videos, tutorials, and other content related to my work and hobbies. I've done lots of work on cars, from custom headlights, tailights, gauge clusters, and headunits to heavy mechanicl work like replacing head gaskets and clutches.
